Nestled on the foothills of the magnificent 90,000-acre D'Agular range and located just 400m from the forest edge, Lacey’s Creek Homestead and Vineyard has withstood the test of time. This century-old wooden homestead, settled in 1889, boasts a rich history and has been a cherished retreat for those seeking solitude and a glimpse into the past, when life was lived at a slower pace, and nature was revered as a sacred entity.


Step back in time and experience the charm of yesteryear with a stay at Lacey's Creek Homestead. Recently renovated to its original glory, this stunning property boasts 12-foot ceilings, beautiful stained glass casement windows, a cosy wood fire heater, and classic timber floors throughout. The meticulous restoration of the original hardware and windows provides an authentic touch and a true sense of what life was like 100 years ago.


As a working farm, guests can expect to immerse themselves in rural life with chickens, ducks, geese and even the occasional escaped goat roaming around the property. With cattle roaming the hills and fields nearby, Laceys Creek Station offers a unique and authentic farm stay experience. Book now and be transported to a different era.

Laceys Creek Homestead & Vineyard


Experience authentic country living at Laceys Creek Homestead — a beautifully restored 1920s Queenslander farmhouse set on 110 acres, just 10 minutes from Dayboro and under an hour from Brisbane.

Perched high on a ridgeline, the homestead offers sweeping valley views, fresh mountain air, and a true taste of rural life. With wide verandahs, timber floors, high ceilings and a cosy fireplace, it combines historic charm with modern comfort — the perfect escape for families, couples, or small groups (sleeps up to 6).

Country Surroundings


Wander the paddocks, say hello to our cattle, chickens and ducks, or enjoy a picnic under the big gums. The property backs onto D’Aguilar National Park — making Laceys Creek Homestead an ideal base for bushwalking, birdwatching, and exploring the Mount Mee forest trails.


Weddings & Local Attractions


If you’re attending a wedding or event at Ocean View Estates or Glengariff Historic Estate, our homestead is just a short drive away (≈15 minutes). We offer flexible check-in and plenty of parking to make your stay stress-free.
Dayboro’s cafes, markets and historic village are nearby, and within an hour you can reach Samford, Mount Glorious, Somerset Dam, and the vineyards of the Moreton Bay hinterland.
